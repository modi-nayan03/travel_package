Travel Tips

Essential information for your Indian adventure

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather across most of India. Avoid monsoon season (June-September) and peak summer (April-May).

Transportation

India has extensive rail and road networks. Book trains in advance, use metro in major cities, and consider domestic flights for long distances.

Local Customs

Dress modestly, remove shoes before entering temples, and respect local traditions. Learn basic Hindi phrases for better communication.
